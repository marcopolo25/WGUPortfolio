In Kingston, Jamaica

A little background about me. I was born in the city of Kingston, which is the capital of Jamaica. My birth was extremely complicated, because I had a deformity of my feet called congenital talipes equinovarus (CTEV) or clubfoot. I am very thankful for the mother God gave me as she percevire to ensure that one day I would be considered normal, and it paid off. After a slew of intense surgeries and rehab, I was finally about to walk at the age of 2. A miracle indeed. I spent 19 years of my life in Jamaica, then I migrated to the United States in pursuit of a higher education.
